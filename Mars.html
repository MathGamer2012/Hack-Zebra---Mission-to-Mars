Distance from the Sun:

The distance between the Sun and Mars is 213.67 million kiliometers!

Size/Age:

The size (radius) of Mars is 3,389 kiliometers! Mars is actually 4.603 billion years old!

Fun Facts:

1. Mars is home to the tallest mountain in the solar system.
2. Only 18 missions to Mars have been successful.
3. Mars has the largest dust storms in the solar system.
4. Pieces of Mars have fallen to Earth.
5. One day Mars will have a ring around it.
