Saturn

Saturn is the 2nd largest planet in the Solar System!

Saturn






Distance from the Sun:

The distance between the Sun and Saturn is 1.493 billion kilometers!

Size/Age:

The size (radius) of Saturn is 58,232 kiliometers! Saturn is actually 4.503 billion years old!

Fun Facts:

1. Saturn is the most distant planet that can be seen with the naked eye.
2. Saturn has more moons than any other planet.
3. Saturn has 150 moons and smaller moonlets.
4. Saturn orbits the Sun once every 29.4 Earth years.
5. Saturn is the flattest planet.
